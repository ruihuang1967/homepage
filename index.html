Rui (Ray) Huang

Senior Research Engineer, Samsung Research America

I am a Senior Research Engineer at Samsung Research America, Standards and Mobility Innovation (SMI) Lab, specializing in R&D for the Next Generation of Wireless Systems.

I received my Ph.D. from The University of British Columbia in 2022, and my M.Sc. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2018.

Summary of Experiences

  • 8 years of Engineering experience in Telecom. systems (5G/6G), focusing on PHY and MAC layers, including beamforming and MIMO solutions deployed in commercial networks.
  • Lead inventor/co-inventor of 15+ US/WO patents with 3GPP Standardization impact.
